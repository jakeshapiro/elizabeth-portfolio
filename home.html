Welcome

Elizabeth Hansen Shapiro

Nonprofit Executive | Journalism Researcher | Local News Advocate

Elizabeth Hansen Shapiro is a nonprofit executive and academic dedicated to the future of local journalism. She co-founded the National Trust for Local News and served as its founding CEO from 2021 to 2025, leading efforts to acquire and sustain local newspapers as nonprofit community assets.

She holds a PhD in Organizational Behavior and Sociology from Harvard Business School and is currently a Senior Research Fellow at the Tow Center for Digital Journalism at Columbia University. Her research focuses on news sustainability, the future of public media, and public policies to support local news.

Previously, Elizabeth led news sustainability research at the Shorenstein Center on Media, Politics, and Public Policy at Harvard Kennedy School. Her work bridges academic research and practical solutions to address the local news crisis facing communities across America.
