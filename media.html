Swarthmore College Magazine | Fall 2024 | By Christine Buckley

Strength in Storytelling — One Community at a Time

A profile of Elizabeth's work co-founding the National Trust for Local News, tracing the personal and intellectual origins of her mission to reimagine local press as an investment in democracy — from her grandmother's community newspaper column to leading a nonprofit that now owns more than 65 papers across the country.

Breaking News

An in-depth feature on Elizabeth's novel funding and ownership model for the National Trust for Local News, exploring how the Colorado acquisition offers a replicable path to keeping local newspapers in local hands — and sustained as civic assets for the future.
